Transportation

School transportation is available for students in most areas of Dubai. For information about bus routes and schedules, please contact our transportation department at +971-4-2634083 or email transport@alsadiqschool.com.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the school hours?

School hours are from 7:45 AM to 2:30 PM for all grades, Sunday through Thursday. The campus opens at 7:00 AM and students should arrive no later than 7:40 AM for morning assembly.

How can I apply for admission?

To apply for admission, you can visit our Admissions page to download the application form or apply online. You can also visit our Admissions Office during working hours. For specific questions, contact our Admissions Officer at admissions@alsadiqschool.ae.

Does the school provide transportation?

Yes, we provide transportation services to most areas in Dubai. Transportation fees vary depending on the location. For more information, please contact our Transportation Department.

What curriculum does the school follow?

Al Sadiq Islamic English School follows the British National Curriculum, enhanced with Islamic education and Arabic language studies in compliance with UAE Ministry of Education requirements.
