About Al Sadiq Islamic English School

Our History

Al Sadiq Islamic English School was established in 1989 with a vision to provide quality education that combines academic excellence with Islamic values and teachings. Starting with just a handful of students, our school has grown to become a respected educational institution serving hundreds of families in our community.

For over two decades, we have maintained our commitment to nurturing young minds with knowledge, wisdom, and strong moral character. Our graduates have gone on to prestigious universities around the world, carrying with them the values and principles instilled during their time at Al Sadiq.
